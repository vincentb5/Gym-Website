Step 5: Recovery & Nutrition

Recovery is essential for progress and injury prevention. Muscle growth and strength gains occur when your body repairs itself after workouts. Prioritize quality sleep, stay hydrated, and eat a balanced diet that includes enough protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Listening to your body and taking rest days when needed will help you stay consistent and continue improving over time.

Summary:

Support muscle growth and performance through rest, hydration, and balanced nutrition.
